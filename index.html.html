Pattduru Homestay – A Serene Retreat in the Heart of Chikkamagaluru** Nestled amidst the lush greenery of the Western Ghats, **Pattduru Homestay** near Mudigere, Chikkamagaluru, offers a perfect getaway for nature lovers and adventure seekers alike. Surrounded by misty hills, sprawling coffee plantations, and vibrant wildlife, this homestay provides a tranquil escape from the hustle and bustle of city life. Experience the warmth of traditional Malnad hospitality with cozy, well-maintained rooms, delicious home-cooked meals, and breathtaking views from every corner of the property. Whether you're looking to unwind in nature, explore scenic trekking trails, or sip freshly brewed coffee by the bonfire, Pattduru Homestay ensures a truly rejuvenating experience. **Why Choose Pattduru Homestay?**

  • ✔️ Comfortable Stay:** Well-furnished rooms with modern amenities for a cozy and relaxing experience.
  • ✔️ Authentic Malnad Cuisine Savor traditional homemade delicacies prepared with fresh local ingredients.
  • ✔️ Adventure & Activities:** Enjoy nature walks, trekking, plantation tours, and wildlife spotting.
  • ✔️ Bonfire & Stargazing:** Unwind by the bonfire under a starlit sky, making unforgettable memories.
Whether you're traveling with family, friends, or on a solo retreat, **Pattduru Homestay** promises an unforgettable stay filled with nature, comfort, and adventure.
